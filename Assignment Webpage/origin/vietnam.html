Vietnam Cuisine
Vietnamese cuisine is known for its fresh ingredients, light yet flavorful dishes, and balance of textures and flavors.
It often features a combination of sweet, sour, salty, bitter, and umami elements, creating a harmonious dining experience.
Common ingredients include fish sauce, fresh herbs (cilantro, basil, mint), rice noodles, lemongrass, and fermented sauces.
Cooking techniques emphasize steaming, stir-frying, grilling, and slow-simmering to retain the natural flavors of ingredients.
